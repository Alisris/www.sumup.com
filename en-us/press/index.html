SumUp Story

Starting your own business shouldn’t be difficult. Since 2012, SumUp has worked towards its vision of a world where independent small business owners can take the hassle out of getting paid, and focus on doing what they love. By providing powerful and affordable tools, more than 3 million businesses of all sizes choose SumUp to accept their payments both in-store and online.

In early 2020 SumUp further expanded its full product suite and 360° payment solution by launching Invoicing, Gift Cards, and Mobile Payments, offering even more ways for small businesses to engage with their customers. For any press inquiries, please get in touch with us: [email protected]
