What is a loyalty rewards program?

Long gone are the days where consumers are expected to keep track of a physical punch card. Now, shoppers prefer an easier way to earn points for their purchases. With a powerful loyalty rewards program like SumUp Connect, your customers can seamlessly sign up to join your digital program right at checkout. From then on, they can sign in with their phone number to redeem rewards. 

Once a customer is in your database, we’ll send automatic promotions to keep them engaged with your business. Plus, we’ll also market to other consumers in the SumUp network to promote your business, growing your customer base easier than ever.

The importance of loyalty for your business 

Did you know that on average, 60% of customers don't return to a business after their first visit? With our digital loyalty rewards program, you can easily keep track of customer rewards, send automated marketing promotions, and so much more.

Start growing with SumUp Connect

Automate your rewards

With automated marketing, you can craft a digital rewards program for your customers and build a set of automatically sent campaigns to welcome new customers and bring back those who haven’t visited recently.

Launch impactful campaigns

The AutoPilot feature automatically sends your customers an app notification, text message, or email with special offers that bring them back 10x more effectively than other marketing tools.

Track customer activity

Every time a customer signs up, checks in at the counter, or redeems a reward, your dashboard will track that activity. Best of all, AutoPilot and Promotions work together to bring your customers back faster.

Build your network

There are over 70 million shoppers who use the Fivestars App to find local businesses. When new customers find you through the app, they’re even offered an introductory promotion to get them excited about visiting your business!

Customize audience groups

With our Segmentation Tool, you can create customized categories that make sense for your business. When you’re ready to send a promotion, simply select the groups you want to reach and watch your message make an impact.

In-App and website promotion

Through the Fivestars App or website, users can explore an interactive map of nearby businesses, allowing maximum exposure of your business to potential customers.

Grow your business faster than ever

Loyalty & Retention

When you sign up customers for your loyalty program, they’re added to your customer database. From there, we’ll keep track of the points they earn and automatically send them rewards based on where they are in their journey with your business.

Customer Acquisition

Your business will be featured on the Customer Touchscreen at neighboring (and non-competing) businesses. We show the reward you’ve set up for Acquisition, and once interested customers save it, they’re added to your database.

Powerful Promotions

Personalized promotions are a great way to connect with your customers. That’s why we created customer groups based on their in-store behavior, so you can send different promotions to new or existing customers.

Marketing Automation

AutoPilot is the tool at the heart of SumUp Connect, sending your customers the targeted offers they’ll love—totally automatically. We designed AutoPilot specifically for businesses to deliver the perfectly-timed nudges that get customers coming back and spending more.

Text-to-Join Capability

With the text-to-join feature, customers can text your business-specific keyword to 578277 (5STARS) and they’ll be instantly added to your membership list. Easily post this code in your storefront, on social media, or website.

Optimized Tracking

Through your loyalty dashboard, you’ll always have access to intuitively organized and easily navigable insights on how your marketing program is performing, on both a big-picture and a granular level.
