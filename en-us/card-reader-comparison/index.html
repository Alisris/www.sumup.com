A card reader for every business

You should be able to accept payments for your small business on your terms. That’s why each of our card readers were created around three main qualities.

Portable

Whether you’re using a WiFi connection or a device with built-in mobile data, you can use all of our card readers wherever you do business.

Efficient

Each card reader is easy to set up and even easier to use. Once your SumUp Account has been approved, you can start taking payments right away.

Multifunctional

You can do everything from creating catalogs to tracking transactions in the free SumUp App or directly from your card reader’s screen.

Questions? We’ve got you covered.

How do I set up and use my SumUp Card Reader?

Whether you’ve chosen the Plus, Plus Bundle, or Solo Card Reader, believe that using your SumUp Card Reader should be simple and intuitive. Check out the SumUp Help Center to find step-by-step setup guides and helpful troubleshooting tips.

Do I need an additional device to use my card reader?

The Solo Card Reader is a standalone device, so you don’t need to pair the reader with your smartphone or tablet in order to track or process your payments. The Plus Card Reader is the only reader that needs an additional device in order to accept payments. Learn more about using the Plus with your smart devices here.

If you’re using an iOS device, you’ll need to make sure it has Bluetooth 4.0 and its operating system is iOS 12 or higher. On an Android device, you’ll need Bluetooth 4.0 and its operating system must be Android 6.x or higher.

How much do payments cost with a SumUp Card Reader?

All SumUp Card Readers are free from monthly and hardware costs. You only have to pay a standard 2.6% + 10¢ transaction fee whenever you take an in-person payment using your device.

What cards do SumUp Card Readers accept?

All SumUp Card Readers and products accept major debit and credit cards, as well as NFC payment options. Choose from contactless, chip or swipe. You can find a full list of accepted cards here:

  • Visa

  • Mastercard

  • American Express

  • Discover

  • Diners Club

  • Google Pay

  • Apple Pay

I’ve ordered my SumUp Card Reader. What happens now?

Once you’ve ordered your device, you’ll want to make sure you’ve completed your SumUp Account set up and business registration. Your device will arrive within 4-5 business days but won’t be able accept payments until your registration is complete and approved by our team.
