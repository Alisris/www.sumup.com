Business Bank Account* FAQs

What do I need to open a business account?

Before we can open your SumUp Business Account, we first need to verify your identity. All you’ll need to do is upload a photo of an ID document, such as a valid driving license or passport.

We may also ask you to provide a utility bill or bank statement too, as a means of verifying your address.

How fast can I open a business account?

It usually takes less than 10 minutes to open a business account with SumUp. Once you’ve signed up and verified your identity, your account number is ready to use right away, so you can instantly add money and make transfers while your prepaid debit card is on its way.

On rare occasions, it might take us a little longer to verify your documents, especially if the photo of your ID document is blurry or if the address you entered doesn’t match the one on your proof of address.

Is SumUp a bank?*

SumUp is a leading financial services provider and an authorized electronic money institution. This enables us to issue secure e-money accounts with our partner bank, account numbers, routing numbers, and a free SumUp Mastercard® Debit Card* for your small business.

How long does it take to get my free SumUp Mastercard® Debit Card* in the mail?

Your SumUp Mastercard® Debit Card* typically arrives within 3-5 business days. To ensure your card is delivered to the correct address, review the address associated with your SumUp Account before requesting your card.

How can I view my Business Bank Account* details?

Currently, your SumUp Business Bank Account* can only be accessed from the SumUp App on any iOS device. Soon, you’ll be able to access your banking information from the online SumUp Dashboard and Android devices.

How do I fund my Business Bank Account?*

You can fund your account through a wire transfer, or through a third-party consumer payment platform such as Venmo or Cashapp. Additionally, you can also change your payout settings to your SumUp Business Bank Account and receive all your payouts directly to your SumUp Business Account.

Where can I find more information about my SumUp Business Bank Account?*

You can find more information about pricing, fees, account limits, and more,  by visiting our Help Center.

How much does it cost to open a business account?

Opening a business account with SumUp is 100% free. We also don’t charge a monthly or annual fee, or a fee to send or receive money. See our fee schedule above.*
