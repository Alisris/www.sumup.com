What is a POS System?

A POS system (point of sale system) is the hardware used to process payments and orders, as well as the software running on those systems. ‘Point of sale’ refers to the time and place a customer interacts with a merchant to purchase services or goods.

Besides processing payments, POS systems – such as Point of Sale Lite – usually allow the merchant to take care of back office tasks, like analyzing transaction reports and managing their items and prices from one single platform.

How do I set up my Point of Sale Lite

Setting up Point of Sale Lite is easy! Just follow these simple steps:

  • Unbox the device

  • Connect it to its mains cable

  • Turn it on using the power button below the SumUp logo

  • Follow the instructions to sign into your SumUp profile

  • If you don’t have a profile yet, first go to sumup.me using your computer, then log in

  • Now you have access to all the features. Get started by creating an item catalog with all your items or access your existing item catalog saved in your SumUp profile

  • To take card payments, simply add an item to your shopping cart and select the payment method “Card Reader”, then follow the instructions

Do I need a card reader to accept payments on Point of Sale Lite?

Yes, you do need a SumUp card reader to accept card payments together with Point of Sale Lite. The bundle includes a SumUp Solo card reader.

What does Point of Sale Lite cost? Are there recurring charges?

The Point of Sale Lite comes with no subscription and no monthly charges. Simply buy the hardware for $499 and pay the usual 2.60% + 10¢ transaction fee when you accept payments with your card reader.

Which card types does the Point of Sale Lite accept?

Point of Sale Lite and all SumUp Card Readers and products accept major debit and credit card brands, and NFC payment options such as Google Pay and Apple Pay. The SumUp Solo Card Reader accepts both contactless and chip cards. You can find a full list of accepted cards here.

Which receipt printers can I connect to my Point of Sale Lite?

Point of Sale Lite is compatible with receipt printers from Star Micronics and Epson that are able to communicate over USB, Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. Please note that the Solo Printer can currently not be used with Point of Sale Lite.
