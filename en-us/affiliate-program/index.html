Questions? We've got you covered.

What is the SumUp Affiliate Program and how does it work?

The Affiliate Program is a service that allows participating websites to earn commissions for different conversion actions, such as leads, sign ups, and sales referred to SumUp using tracking links. When a visitor you have referred to SumUp converts on eligible conversions we keep track of it and give you a commission on this event.

What is the SumUp Local Partners Program and how does it work?

Our Local Partners help us support the small businesses in their communities. We reward these connections through compensation for referrals, meetings, and contracts with new merchants.

Is my website eligible for the Affiliate Program

We accept all types of websites that are relevant to our products. However, we do have guidelines for the types of businesses we can support. Please refer to our Help Center for a complete list of prohibited business types. We accept all applications, however reserve the right to refuse or revoke membership at any time.

Am I eligible to be a Local Partner?

Yes! Anyone who has connections with local small businesses is welcome to join the Local Partners program. Sign up at the link above to get started.

How much can I earn?

There is no limit on how much you can earn. As an affiliate, the more business owners that visit our website and purchase a product via your personalized link, the more you earn. Similarly, as a local partner, the more owners you refer to purchase SumUp products the more money you can make.

Do I have to commit to advertise SumUp products in a specific way?

Absolutely not! We want you to act independently and decide for yourself on how to advertise the SumUp products. Feel free to add your personal opinion about our products.
