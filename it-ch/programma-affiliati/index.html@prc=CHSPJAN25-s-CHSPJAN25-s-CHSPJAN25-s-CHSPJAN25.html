What is SumUp?

SumUp is a simple solution for anyone who needs to process cashless payments.

Our card readers are highly affordable, with no monthly costs or binding contracts.

Merchants simply pay a low fixed fee per transaction. 

As a SumUp Sales Affiliate, you’ll earn a market-leading commission for each SumUp card reader that is bought via your referral link.

Frequently asked questions

Can anyone become a SumUp Affiliate?

The Program focuses mostly on large content portals that are relevant to the SumUp products (e.g. FinTech blogs and card reader comparison websites). Please refer to section 1.1 of the SumUp Affiliate Terms and Conditions for a list of unsuitable websites.

How much can I earn?

There is no limit on how much you can earn. The more visitors you refer to our website and who buy a device, the more you’ll earn. Commissions are paid on a Cost-per-Order model. Please be aware that commissions will be paid only when the total due amount reaches a minimum of €250

Do I have to commit to advertise SumUp products in a specific way?

Absolutely not! We want you to act independently and decide for yourself on how to advertise the SumUp products. Feel free to add your personal opinion about our products - negative as well as positive.
