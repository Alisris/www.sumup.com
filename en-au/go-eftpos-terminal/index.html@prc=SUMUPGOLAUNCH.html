FAQs

What is the SumUp Go?

It’s a portable, standalone EFTPOS terminal suitable for fixed-location merchants and mobile sellers. It offers an all-day battery life, a durable form factor along with free and unlimited cellular connectivity, making it a great option for mobile checkouts.

What are the fees for using the SumUp Go?

Transparency and affordability are important to us. All SumUp terminals are contract-free, require no paperwork, and have no extra fees. You’ll only be charged a fee of 1.60% whenever a transaction is made.

How much does the SumUp Go cost?

As a standalone card reader device, the Go is an all-in-one terminal which can efficiently manage your transaction needs at an affordable price. You can buy your card reader for a once-off fee of $195 $239*.

Does the SumUp Go have a surcharge feature?

Yes, the Go offers surcharging capabilities through the SumUp app. Visit our Support Centre to learn how to set up surcharging.

How do I accept credit card payments?

This terminal accepts most major payment methods, including contactless, chip & PIN, Google Pay and Apple Pay. Thanks to its easy-to-use form factor, you can simply hand the terminal to your customer when they’re ready to pay.

Is the SumUp Go right for small businesses?

As an all-in-one payment solution, the SumUp Go is the perfect device for small businesses, or businesses where checkout mobility is important. Better yet, this standalone device accepts most major payment methods. It’s compact, portable and durable, and thanks to its long battery life and free mobile internet, you’ll be ready to sell anywhere in seconds.
