FAQs

What is an invoice?

An invoice is a sales document that a business sends to its customer. It lists the products and services that the business has provided and acts as an official request for payment for the provided goods or services.

Depending on your country's regulations, invoicing is mandatory for certain sales. This mainly includes business-to-business transactions and transactions between VAT registered businesses.

What is SumUp Invoices?

SumUp Invoices is an easy-to-use invoicing software. You can create invoices, quotes and other sales documents and send them directly to your customer's email. Conveniently, with SumUp Invoices, you can create invoices on your computer or your mobile phone, allowing you to do your invoicing from the comfort of your own home or on the go.

What do I include on an invoice?

SumUp Invoices makes sure that all the required information is included before you send the invoice to your customer. Typically, invoices should include the following:

  • A unique invoice number

  • Your company’s name, address and contact details

  • Your customer’s name and address

  • A description of the goods or services provided

  • The date these goods or services were provided

  • The invoice date

  • The cost of the goods or services

  • The VAT amount (if applicable)

  • The total amount owed

How much does SumUp Invoices cost?

We offer two different pricing plans: SumUp Invoices Free and SumUp Invoices Pro.

The Free plan is great for smaller businesses and includes unlimited invoices and quotes. The Pro plan is £7 per month on the yearly plan and is geared toward growing companies that require invoice customisation, reduced online payment fees, invoice translation and automation features.

Learn more on the pricing page.

What features are included in SumUp Invoices?

Our goal is to make your life easier. We offer several features to help you automate and simplify your workflow, including recurring invoices, invoice designer, automatic payment reminders, bank transfers and real-time notifications.
