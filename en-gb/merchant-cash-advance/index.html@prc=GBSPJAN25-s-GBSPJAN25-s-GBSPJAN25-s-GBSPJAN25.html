Questions about SumUp Cash Advance?

Taking out a Cash Advance is a big decision. Lets make sure all your open questions have been addressed.

Can anyone apply for funding from SumUp Cash Advance?

In short, no. Only merchants who are offered a SumUp Cash Advance can request funding. If you're eligible to request a Cash Advance, well get in touch with you directly, so make sure youre subscribed to our marketing emails and have our app notifications turned on so that you dont miss your offer.

If you have not received an offer yet, make sure to check our eligibility criteria to increase your odds.

How do I manage my SumUp Cash Advance?

Just log in to the SumUp Dashboard and click "Cash Advance". Here you can see your transactions, the amount youve already paid back, the amount left to pay and when this needs to be paid back by. You can also make additional repayments via your SumUp dashboard.

Into which bank account will my cash advance be paid out?

Your cash advance will be wired to the bank account associated with your SumUp profile within 2 working days. Please remember to keep any third party bank account you have registered with SumUp updated to avoid issues with your cash advance payout.

When do I have to pay the cash advance back?

Youll need to pay back the full Cash Advance amount plus the fixed fee within the agreed repayment period as specified in our Terms and Conditions, which begins the day the Cash Advance is transferred to your bank account. Once weve transferred the money, well begin deducting the fixed percentage from your SumUp card sales. Youll need to make a minimum monthly payment that is also shown in your Cash Advance offer.

Can I repay the cash advance earlier?

Yes. If you want to make a one-off repayment to settle your Cash Advance, you can contact our support team via phone or email, and well arrange a flexible repayment for you with no additional fee. You can also make use of the Self Service feature in your Lending dashboard to make a payment.

What happens if I cant repay my Cash Advance on time?

Were here to support you in difficult times. If youre struggling to make your repayments or think you may struggle in the future then you can get in touch with our Cash Advance experts at [email protected]. You can also call us on 03308 083887 to discuss your repayment options.

What is a personal guarantee?

Not all merchants are required to complete a Personal Guarantee; we assess this on a case-by-case basis.

A personal guarantee is a legally binding agreement where the guarantor agrees to be personally responsible for the debt if the borrower defaults. This means that if you sign a personal guarantee and the borrower defaults, the lender can take legal action against you to recover the unpaid debt, so it is crucial to understand the terms and potential consequences before signing and seek legal advice if needed.

How will I receive my Personal Guarantee?

Please follow the instructions that will be sent to you via email from our Docusign account, you will receive it within 24 hrs after accepting the Terms and Conditions. In case you do not receive this email, please contact us by sending an email to [email protected] or by calling us on 03308083887.
