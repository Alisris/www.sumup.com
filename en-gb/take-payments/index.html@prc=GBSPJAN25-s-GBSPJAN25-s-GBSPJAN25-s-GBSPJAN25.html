FAQs

How do I set up and use my SumUp card reader?

Whether you’ve opted for the Air or the Solo, we believe that using your SumUp card reader should be simple and intuitive. To help you get started, we’ve collected a number of easy-to-follow guides to help you start accepting card payments as quickly as possible.

How do I take card payments with my SumUp product?

It’s really easy to take card payments with our products. You can accept either chip&PIN or contactless payments on your Air or Solo from the following cards:

  • Visa

  • V. PAY

  • Mastercard

  • American Express

  • Google Pay

  • Apple Pay

With Tap to Pay, you can accept contactless payments by asking your customer to hold their card over the contactless symbol at the top of the screen. Please note, with Tap to Pay on iPhone, you can accept Visa and Mastercard payments, as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay and other digital wallets. American Express payments are currently not supported for Tap to Pay on iPhone transactions.

I’ve ordered my SumUp product, what happens now?

Great, you’ve taken the first step towards taking payments for your business with ease. Once you’ve placed your order for an Air or Solo and we’ve received your payment, we’ll process your order and dispatch your device. We’ll send you a confirmation message and a tracking link via SMS and email so that you can keep an eye on its progress. If you’ve chosen Tap to Pay, you can get started straight away. Simply follow the links to create your SumUp profile, download the free SumUp app and log in. On your homescreen you’ll see ‘Tap to Pay.’

How much does it cost to take payments with SumUp?

You can choose to either pay-as-you-go or subscribe with SumUp One. If you pay-as-you-go, you’ll have no monthly costs and only have to pay a standard transaction fee of 1.69% whenever you take a payment using your card reader or Tap to Pay. If you subscribe to SumUp One, you’ll pay a monthly fee of £19, but you’ll benefit from only paying 0.99% transaction fee on each payment you take.

What payment methods can I accept with SumUp Tap to Pay?

With Tap to Pay on iPhone, you can accept Visa, Mastercard, AMEX and Maestro payments, as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay and other digital wallets.

With Tap to Pay on Android, you can accept Visa, Mastercard and Maestro payments, as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay and other digital wallets. AMEX is not currently supported for Tap to Pay on Android.

Which phones are compatible with SumUp’s Tap to Pay products?

To use Tap to Pay on iPhone you’ll need to make sure that you’re using an iPhone XS or newer which is running the latetst iOS.

To use Tap to Pay on Android you’ll need to be running Android 8 or higher.

Both Tap to Pay on iPhone and Android require you to download the free SumUp app to use.

Which account will my money be paid into?

You have two options to receive payouts: either the free SumUp Business Account or your own bank account.

The perk of the Business Account? You’ll always receive your payouts the very next day – even on weekends and bank holidays. No extra cost, no catch. Plus, you'll get a free Mastercard for all your business expenses. Learn more about the free SumUp Business Account.

How long does it take for the money to appear in my account?

Your money will appear in your free SumUp Business Account the very next day – even on weekends and bank holidays. You can then spend or withdraw your money using your complementary Mastercard. 

If you prefer, you can opt to receive payouts directly to any other bank account, with funds arriving within 1 to 2 business days.
