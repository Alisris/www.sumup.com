FAQs

How do I set up and use my SumUp card reader?

Whether you’ve opted for the Air or the Solo, we believe that using your SumUp card reader should be simple and intuitive. To help you get started, we’ve collected a number of easy-to-follow guides to help you start accepting card payments as quickly as possible.

How do I charge my SumUp card reader?

We know that keeping your card reader ready to go is essential to running your business. That’s why all of our devices are easy to charge. If you’ve purchased the Solo, you'll find a charging station already included so you keep your card reader fully charged at all times. If you’ve opted for the Solo and printer, your printing device also acts as a charging station. The Air reader is available to buy with a charging station too. Or, you can keep it fully juiced by attaching the included Micro USB cable to a computer or power source on a regular basis.

What devices are compatible with SumUp card readers?

As the Solo card reader is a standalone device, you don’t need to pair it with your smartphone in order to track or process your payments. However, you do need to download the SumUp app to sign up for an account. The app is also a great place to explore our other remote payment options.

The Air card reader does need to be connected to your mobile device in order to take payments so it has certain compatibility requirements to connect. If you’re using an iOS device, you’ll need to make sure it has Bluetooth 4.0 and its operating system is iOS 12 or higher. On an Android device, you’ll need Bluetooth 4.0 and its operating system must be Android 6.x or higher. You can find out more information about compatibility in our simple guide.

How much do payments cost with a SumUp card reader?

All of SumUp’s card readers are free from binding contracts and monthly costs. You only have to pay a standard 1.69% transaction fee whenever you take a payment using your device.

What cards do SumUp card readers accept?

All SumUp card readers and products accept major debit and credit card brands, as well as NFC payment options. Choose from contactless, Chip and PIN, or look into our other products for remote payment options. You can find a full list of accepted cards here:

  • Visa

  • V Pay

  • Mastercard

  • Maestro

  • American Express

  • Discover

  • Diners Club

  • Union Pay

  • Google Pay

  • Apple Pay

I’ve ordered my SumUp card reader. What happens now?

Excellent, you’ve taken the first step towards taking payments for your business with ease. Once you’ve placed your order and we’ve received your payment, we’ll process your order and dispatch your device. We’ll send you a confirmation message and a tracking link via SMS and email so that you can keep an eye on its progress. The standard delivery time is 2 to 3 business days, so you won’t have long to wait. You can find out more information about shipping in our handy support guide.
