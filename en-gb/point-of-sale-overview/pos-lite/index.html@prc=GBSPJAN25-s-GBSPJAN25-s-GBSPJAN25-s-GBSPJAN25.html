FAQs

What is a POS system?

A POS system (point of sale system) is the hardware used to process payments and orders, as well as the software running on those systems. ‘Point of sale’ refers to the time and place a customer interacts with a merchant to purchase services or goods.

Besides processing payments, POS systems – such as Point of Sale Lite – usually allow the merchant to take care of administrative tasks, like analysing transaction reports and managing their items and prices from one single platform.

How do I set up my Point of Sale Lite?

Setting up Point of Sale Lite couldn’t be easier. Just follow these simple steps:

  • Unbox the device

  • Connect it to its mains cable

  • Turn it on using the power button below the SumUp logo

  • Follow the instructions to sign into your SumUp profile

  • If you don’t have a profile yet, first go to sumup.me using your computer, then log in

  • Now you have access to all the features – get started by creating an item catalogue with all your items or access your existing item catalogue saved in your SumUp profile

  • To take card payments, simply add an item to your shopping cart and select the payment method “Card Reader”, then follow the instructions

Do I need a card reader to accept card payments with Point of Sale Lite?

Yes, you do need a SumUp card reader to accept card payments together with Point of Sale Lite.

Currently, Point of Sale Lite is compatible with the SumUp Solo card reader. If you don’t have a Solo card reader yet, you can get it here. If you already have a Solo card reader, you can purchase the standalone Point of Sale Lite tablet here.

Can I connect Point of Sale Lite to my existing SumUp profile?

Yes, if you already have a SumUp profile, simply log in with your credentials when turning on your POS Lite. 

Once logged in, you have immediate access to your SumUp profile, your personal item catalogue, your sales history and the SumUp Business Account.

What does it cost? Are there any recurring charges?

Unlike most POS systems, Point of Sale Lite comes with no subscription and no monthly charges. Simply buy the hardware for £349* and pay the usual 1.69 % transaction fee when you accept payments with your card reader.

Which receipt printer can I connect to Point of Sale Lite?

Point of Sale Lite is compatible with receipt printers from Star Micronics and Epson that are able to communicate over USB, Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. Please note that the Solo Printer can currently not be used with Point of Sale Lite.

To see whether your printer is compatible, take a look at the full list of compatible receipt printers in the Support Centre.
