SumUp Story

Anyone should be able to start their own business and getting paid shouldnt be a hassle. Since 2012, SumUp has worked towards its vision of a world where independent business owners can be successful doing what they love. By providing powerful and affordable tools, such as our innovative card readers and the SumUp Card for even faster payouts, more than 4 million businesses of all sizes now choose SumUp for payments

both in-store and online. In early 2020 SumUp further expanded its full product suite and 360 payment solution by launching Invoicing, Gift Cards, Mobile Payments and Online Store, offering even more ways for small businesses to engage with their customers.
