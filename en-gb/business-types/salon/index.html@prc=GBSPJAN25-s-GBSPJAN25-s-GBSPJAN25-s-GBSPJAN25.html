FAQs

Looking for answers? Here are some of the most common questions asked by businesses like yours.

Can I accept card payments on the go?

Yes, you can. SumUp's portable card readers and Tap to Pay allow you to take payments anywhere, without needing bulky hardware.

What payment methods can my business accept with SumUp?

With SumUp card readers, you can accept all major debit and credit cards, as well as contactless payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

You can find a full list of accepted payment methods here:

  • Visa

  • V Pay

  • Mastercard

  • Maestro

  • American Express

  • Discover

  • Diners Club

  • Union Pay

  • Google Pay

  • Apple Pay

How can I keep track of payments and invoices?

SumUp’s central dashboard helps you manage all your transactions, whether you're accepting card payments or sending invoices. All your data is stored in one place for easy access.

What happens if my business is closed for a few days?

If you opt for SumUp’s pay-as-you-go, you will incur no monthly charges and you will pay a standard 1.69% for card reader payments. So if your business is closed, you won’t incur any fees.

In case you’re accepting more than £2,800 a month, you can save money with our SumUp One subscription. Find out more about our pricing options.

How long does it take for the money to appear in my account?

Your money will appear in your account the next working day by 4 pm.

You also want to receive next day access to your earnings on weekends and bank holidays? Use the free SumUp Business Account as your payout account – and get next-day access to your earnings by 7 am.
